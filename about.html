One Stop Info Shop

Hello, thanks for checkout out our application! One Stop Info Shop is a simple tool designed to let a user retrieve a Wikipedia summary and top news stories based on a given interest, social issue, or other similar keyword. The user may then enter and save some notes about the current keyword if they wish.
